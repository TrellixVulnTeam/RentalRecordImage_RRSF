About Us

At Rental Record Image, we are committed to helping any company that rents, leases or moves tangible assets — car and truck rental, construction and heavy equipment rental, freight and logistics, and property management — collect best-in-class condition records of those assets to decrease damage disputes and costs. By leveraging new trends in mobile apps, digital documentation and the near ubiquity of mobile devices, Rental Record Image lets customers do away with paper documentation forms, streamline their condition review process and boost the customer service experience.
